The taxicab of China, better known as the jinrickshaw. Equipped with
regular buggy wheels and good springs, these little vehicles are not at all
uncomfortable, and the "horse" is able to make a fair rate of speed. The
runner wears a Chinese raincoat of grass.
Dueling retains some of its popularity in Japan, but it is not carried out with fencing foils, swords or pistols. The participants
wield heavy bamboo rods, with which they try to bowl each other over. The duelists are protected with paaded helmets, face masks,
heavy mittens and breast pads.
