Neither to enroll members nor to
raise funds, but merely to inform the
public "of everything that is to be
known regarding Palestine," is the
avowed aim of "Palestine week,"
which will be held throughout the
I'nited States February 1 to 8.
One of the chief features of this
drive, arranged by the department of
education of the Zionist organization
of America, is to be the distribution
of Palestinian fruits to Jewish chil
dren on the celebration of "the birth
day of the trees," February 4. The
need of new planting in Palestine will be
urged.
Young men's and women's Hebrew
associations, settlements, college or
ganizations, clubs and synagogues
will bring home to America during
the week the salient features regard
ing Palestine and the work or the
Zionist organization.
The week selected marks the com
ing of spring in the Holy l,and.
