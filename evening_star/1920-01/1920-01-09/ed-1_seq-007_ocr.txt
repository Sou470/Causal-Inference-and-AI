Forbidden by the Police.

clubs of Paris have never been so
prosperous, according to competent

forbidden by the police, they flourish

drawals. Even in Holidays.
PARIS. January S.?Frenchmen have
not lost their ability to save money.

that even during the holidays sums
deposited exceeded those withdrawn
by almost exactly 1,000.000 francs.
Records indicate the number of de
