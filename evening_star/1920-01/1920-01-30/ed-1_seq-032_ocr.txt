INCOME TAX WARNING.
Correct Amounts Must Be in Hands
of Collectors by March 15.
Commissioner Roper of the internal
revenue bureau announced today
tentative returns on income taxes, in
use for several years, had been abol
ished. Taxpayers, in making a report
on 1919 incomes, will not be permitted
to estimate their incomes or their
taxes. In making this announcement
Mr. Roper warned that correct amount
of taxes due must be in the hands of
revenue collectors by March 15.
Arrangements have been made by in
ternal revenue officials, however, to
take care of exceptional cases. Tf the
taxpayer is unable to complete his tax
return within the time limit, for vari
ous reasons, authority has been given
for a thirty-day extension of titne.
