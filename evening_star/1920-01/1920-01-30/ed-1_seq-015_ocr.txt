AN URGENT APPEAL'
.The recent appeal of the joint finance committee of the As
sociated Charities and the Citizens' Relief Association for $21,000
has brought returns of $6,000 up to date. Fifteen thousand dollars
is the minimum amount still required by these family social service
agencies to see them through. Their workers are standing guard
against suffering and material distress in volume above the usual
because of the widespread prevalence of influenza. Wage-earners
who are trying to support families on incomes barely sufficient to
make ends meet in prosperous times are being forced to ask for aid
because of prostrating sickness.
Contributions may be designated for either charity or for both,
according to their respective needs.
