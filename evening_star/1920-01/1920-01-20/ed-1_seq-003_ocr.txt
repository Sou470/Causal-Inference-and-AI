claimed a greater number of victims
than influenza.
Dr. Edwin Godfrey, epidemiologist
of the New York state health de
partment, is on his way to Chicago to
study the epidemics here. Dr. Robert
son was informed.

2.279 new cases of influenza were re
ported. and the deaths numbered 26.
