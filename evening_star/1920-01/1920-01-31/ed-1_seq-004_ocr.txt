With the publication of this volume, the jealously guarded
treaties of the Triple Alliance are at last accessible to all who
wish a first-hand knowledge of the diplomatic background of
the World War. The treaties and agreements given here were
found by Prof. Franzis Pribram of the University of Vienna in
the State Archives of Austria: both French and German orig
inals as well as an English translation are included. A second
volume will shortly follow with Prof. Pribram's story of the
negotiations that preceded the signatures of the five treaties of
the Triple Alliance.
