LOCKED POUCH STOLEN,
SLIT; LETTERS RIFLED
Post office inspectors were working
today on the theft of a locked pouch
containing approximately 200 letters
stolen evidently from the Union sta
tion January 11. Postmaster Chance
stated today that the pouch was one
of six delivered by the city post office
January 10 and 11 to the Washington
Terminal Company, and for which the
city post office received receipt.
The pouch was found Saturday In
front of a house in the 1100 block on
1st street. The pouch lock was un
touched, but the bag had been slit,
and all the letters rifled, and then
stuffed back into the pouch. The
pouch contained no registered matter.
