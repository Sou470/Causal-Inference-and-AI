dent Wilson's proposal for settlement
of the strike.
President Lewis and Secretary Green
will go direct to Washington from
Columbus tomorrow. They have been
j summoned to appear in Washington
Monday, along with the other members
of the scale committee of the central
competitive field, to present to the Presi
dent's coal commission, the miner's side
of the controversy over the question of
wages and .working conditions in the
mines. The scale committee will not
have a meeting until it reaches Wash
ington, it is said.
