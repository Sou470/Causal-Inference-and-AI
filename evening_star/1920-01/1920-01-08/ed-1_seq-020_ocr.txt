Each lot of sugar received by us is distributed tn our
stores and priced to you at the usual margin of profit above

one store and at a different price in the same store the next
dav. Or you may find sugar at one price in one store and
at an entirely different price in one of our stores a feu
blocks away, depending entirely on how much time it takes
to sell each lot of sugar in each store.
We are using every means possible to secure sugar to
supply you constantly, and we trust our past reputation
for fair dealing will be a convincing argument that any
price asked by us is entirely justified.
