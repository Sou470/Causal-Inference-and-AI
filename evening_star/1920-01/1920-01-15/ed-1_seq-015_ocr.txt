ANDREW A. SMITH DEAD.
Former Patent Office Employe Suc
cumbs in Chicago.
Andrew Adelbert Smith of Chicaco,
formerly of this city, died Sunday, fol
lowing an operation in Chicago. He
was connected with the patent office
here for ten years.
Mr. Smith was a graduate of the
University of Wisconsin and of George
town University, this city. He left
Washington last April for Chicago to
become associated with the law firm of
Zabel Miller, attorneys. He was con
nected with this firm at the time of his
death.
He Is survived by his wife. Mrs.
Madina Redel Smith, formerly of Madi
son, Wis.; his mother, Mrs. Caroline
Smith of this city; one brother and six
sisters. Interment will be held at
Madison, Wis.
