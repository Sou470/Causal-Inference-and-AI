confirmed the on tire District rent

The action of tlie Senate turns over
to the commission the thousands of
rent controversies between tenants
and landlords for consideration and
decision. The commission is to deter
mine in each case presented to it
what is a fair and reasonable rental
for all classes of rental property, in
cluding business properties, hotels,
apartment houses, boarding houses
and private dwellings.
Saulftliury Act I)ifs in Sixty Day*.
At the end of sixty days from the
date of confirmation of the rent com
mission. the Saulsbury resolution,
passed to prevent profiteering in rents
here during the war, ceases to be op
erative. This does not mean, how
ever. that the rent commission may
not take jurisdiction of all cases im
mediately. The purpose of retaining
the Saulsbury resolution in operation
for sixty days was to give an oppor
tunity to present all contested cases
to the commission.
The commissioners under the act
are authorized to appoint a secretary
and to promulgate such .rul?.s
regulations as they deem wise for the
conduct of the commission's business.
All other employes are to be selected
under civil service rules.
