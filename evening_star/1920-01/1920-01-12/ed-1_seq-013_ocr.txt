FORM TRUCK CORPORATION.
Twenty-Seven Companies in New
York Organize to Relieve Traffic.
NEW YORK, January 12.?Forma
tion of the United States Trucking
Corporation, embodying twenty-seven
trucking companies in Greater New
York, which employ 100.000 men and
represent capital of several million
dollars, is announced here. Interests
of virtually every trucking company
in New York are embraced in the new
organization, it was stated, the merger
being an effort to relieve the city's
traffic and expedite delivery of mer
chandise.
By a system of depots it is planned
to abolish the return of empty trucks
from points to which they were sent
loaded with goods. This method, it is
said, will do away with a large num
ber of trucks that hamper traffic in
congested sections.
