Information received here is to the

league, which has been organizing in
formally in London for many months
past, now is arranging for the selec
tion of permanent headquarters and
is expected to decide within a day or

completed, with special reference to
the needs of the league. Brussels
cannot extend equal accommodations,
but because of its superior conven
ience in the matter of location seems
likely now to be chosen.

'Swiss government has been hesitating
