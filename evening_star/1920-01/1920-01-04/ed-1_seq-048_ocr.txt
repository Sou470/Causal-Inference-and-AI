"An automobile running through the streets
with one or more lights out endangers the life
of every other motorist and every pedestrian.
"A driver operating a machine with front
lights out is likely to be run into by another
driver, who does not see the first machine in
time to avoid accident.
A machine running with the tail light out
is likely to meet with a rear-end collision.
"A lightless automobile running on any high
way is always a menace.
"You will, therefore, assist in the movement
to save human life by enforcing strictly the
police regulation calling for two white lights
on the front of the machine and one red light
in the rear. A machine standing at the curb
may use one of the so-called 'parking lamps.'
"And by all means enforce strictly the regu
lation against dazzling headlights, the kind
which blind every driver on the road coming
in the opposite direction."
