IT would be putting it mildly to say that for
two days after the Jackson night dinner the.

One fact, it may be said, seems to be pretty

tion" is at this time stoutly standing with the
President. The railsplitter ax which Mr. Bryan
swung did not knock off so much as a chip from
the solid trunk of the party organization. If

With a glimmering of foreknowledge of Mr.

pretative reservations to the league of nations.

habitat. That has been his source of strength
