this city, died Wednesday at his resi
dence in East Orange. N. J. He was
forty years old and left Washington six
teen years ago.

this city, lived at 1147 New Hampshire
avenue. He was employed by the SI
Lindsay Rubber Manufacturing Com
pany. He was a member of Foundry
Methodist Episcopal Church.

Jersey and in business was connected
with the wholesale hosiery firm of
Emery-Beers Company, New York. Hi
was a member of Calvary Methodist
Church, East Orange.

Miss Helen MacKinnon; a daughter,
four years of age; his mother. Mir
Emma L. Lindsay, and a brother, Al
fred Lindsay. Funeral services were
held yesterday afternoon at his former
home.
