Reports covering the work and activi- 1
ties of the Merchants and Manufactur
ers' Association during 1919 are to be
presented at the annual meeting of the
association at Rauscher's Tuesday night
by President Joseph A. Berberich and.
Secretary Charles J. Columbus.
The meeting, it is announced, is to be
in the nature of a convention of the
trade sections into which the association
is divided, and chairmen of these sec
tions or units, already elected or to be
named before the annual meeting, are to
be among the speakers. They will re
view the work of the past year in their
own sections and lines of trade and sug
gest plans and lines for co-operation for
further improvement of business condi
tions generally. The annual meeting is
to be followed by a supper.
