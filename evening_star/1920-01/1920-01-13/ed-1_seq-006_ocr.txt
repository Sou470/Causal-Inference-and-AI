half legislation.

by the pending bill merely repudiates
the nation's agreement and obligation
under the organic act, and does not
modify correspondingly the obligation
and sacrifices under the law of the
people of the capital. It gives back to
the nation its promise to pay; it does
not give back to the people of Wash
ington the right of municipal self-tax

which the organic act deprived it. It
retains in Congress, representing the
nation, exclusive power to impose mu
