tenced Smith to pay a fine of $500 and
serve 260 days in jail, and, failing to
pay tho fine, to serve an additional
260 days.

law in such cases. Smith was arrest
ed in a lunchroom Saturday night and
when searched was found to be carry
in* a large revolver, fully loaded.
