TELLS OF WAR-NERVOUS CURE
Dr. T. A. Williams Addresses the
Anthony League.
The weekly meeting of the Anthony
League, 2007 Columbia road, yesterday
was addressed by Dr. Tom A. Wil
liams. He spoke on the practice and
success of the psycho-therapy treat
ment for nervous cases in France re
sulting from the war. He said that
the amount spent by Germany in pen
sions exceeded the total sum spent for
organization.
Dr. Williams in speaking of the suc
cess of the treatment urged its con
tinuance in peace times, declaring
that' it would be a valuable aid to in
dustry. He quoted statistics showing
that not only could men be quickly
cured of nervous trouble resulting
from accidents and injuries, but that
they could be made ready in a short
time to return to their work.
Mrs. Wayne B. Wheeler sang several
songs, accompanied by Mrs. Palmer.
Eight new members were elected to
the association.
