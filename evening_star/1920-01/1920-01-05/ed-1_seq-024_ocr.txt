POLICEMAN RESCUES SKATER.
Uses Long Pole to Save Boy Who
Fell Through Airhole.
Charles Kins, fifteen years old.
skating: on the ice in the Chesapeake
and Ohio canal near the Aqueduct
bridge yesterday afternoon, reached
an airhole in the ice and went
through into the water. His cries
for help attracted Policeman Rubin,
on duty at the north end of the
bridge, and he hurried to the canal.
Cracking of the ice made Rubin
realize the danger in which he was
placed as he walked across the thin
coating on the canal. He procured
a long pole from a watchman on the
bridge and rescued the boy, who was
taken to his home at 3729 Canal
road.
