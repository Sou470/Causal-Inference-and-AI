"The right of the board of education

In any case, if the superintendent says,
'This teacher lacks sound Judgment (or
fails to do her work). I recommend her
dismissal,' and if the board of education

dress under the law.

Question of Loyalty.

"Charges Affecting Character."

."mind. The charges in reality were
