Seaaonal Work a Problem.

lead the way by giving the subject

"It is therefore recommended that

position carrying compensation higher
than the previous compensation; the
act of October 6, 1917, prohibiting
transfers at Increased rates and pro
hibiting salary increases within one
year after transfer.
Federal employes should have the
right to appeal to the Civil Service
Commission. The purpose of this, it
