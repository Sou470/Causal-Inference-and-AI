THE new year ushering in will crowd out the effervescent joys
of the very youthful >et and bring in the official season with its
round of stereotyped dinner parties, when men and women of
the cabinct. of tiie diplomatic corps, of the Supreme Court and of other
branches of officialdom meet and re-meet, dine and re-dine, until the
gamut of prescribed state etiquette is run. The debutantes and charities
have had a right lively fling, the suppressed joyousness of war days
bursting forth with unusual vim. while local institutions that suffered
because their lady bountifuls devoted their time, money and energies
to feeding and clothing the destitute abroad are recouping with telling
energy.
THE diplomatic breakfast at the home of the Secretary of State and
Mrs. Lansing will open the official program, and there will be a
great flurry of days at home, beginning with the first Wednesday in
January and continuing with more or less regularity until Lent, which
this year falls on February 18. The wives of foreign ambassadors and
ministers will observe Friday as a day at home during January and
February, while women of the Senate had the best example possible
set them by Mrs. France of Maryland and Mrs. Spencer of Missouri, who
united in receiving at the Congressional Club a week ago.

will give a dinner in their honor. Mrs. Marshall is tentatively arrang
ing her social program for the season, much depending upon the Presi
dent's health, but she will observe Wednesdays at home with more or
less regularity.
THE White House social program remains practically canceled unless
the President is well enough late in January to admit of some part of
it being carried out. Had all gone well with the President, Mrs. Wilson
would have left one of the most brilliant records behind her ever made
by a mistress Q.f the White House. As it is. nothing could be more
beautiful than her constant devotion in nursing and entertaining the
President. Hundreds of cards pour in upon her, and she is not forgotten
though she may take no part in the social world.
