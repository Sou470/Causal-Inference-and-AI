THE TRAFFIC SQUAD &4 YS:
With the advent of the New Tear pedestrians
and motorists should resolve to assist In prevent
ing accidcnts and saving- human life. Talk street
safety all the time. Talk it especially to the chil
dren. Condemn reckless driving and, above all,
urge obedience to the traffic regulations.
The pedestrian should resolve not to step off
the curb and into the street before looking in
both directions for oncoming traffic; not to walk
in the street except when necessary to cross, and
then only on the regular crosswalks; not to pass
behind street cars without stopping, looking and
listening to make sure that you will not walk in
front of another moving vehicle; not to fall to
watch the traffic officer's signal or semaphore and
then only to move with the traffic at busy corners
and not to fail to tell your friends and relatives
to be careful when in the street and to be care
ful yourself and carry the lesson to others.
The motorist should resolve not to violate
the traffic regulations during the year 1920, espe
cially the speed laws; not to forget to drive more
cautiously at night than in the day time; to co
operate with the traffio officer, who is always
your friend; and to do everything possible to
make the streets of Washington safe.

GAINS FAVOR IN MEXICO
But Natives Are Slow to Accept It
in Elace of Classical
Selections.
MKXICO CITY. December 15 (Corre
spondence of the Associated Press).?
American jazx music, which until re
cently has been received coldly by the
Mexican public, ie Raining in favor
and no dance program or cafe con
cert here is now complete unless it in
cludes three or four selections rem
iniscent of Broadway's whining saxo

reason for the slowness with which

Hands and orchestras abound here.
The Mexican is a natural musician
and the songs that have been written
for him range from the wild rebel
chant of the "La Cucaracha" to the
dreamy, romantic "Beautiful Mexico."
Concerts are given in the parks.
Although termed "popular," they are
made up for the most part of grand
opera selections, and American rag
time as yet has not found a place on
such programs. The Mexican is a
lover of classical music.
