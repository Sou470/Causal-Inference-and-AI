OF HOLY NAME SOCIETY
Trinity Parish Organization De
cides to Observe Anniversary
in January.
A spirited meeting- of the Holy
Name Society of Holy Trinity Church
was staged last night in its rooms in
the new sohoolhouse of the parish.
Reports were made from, committees
in regard to the various activities in
progress.
It was agreed to honor the anni
versary of the Holy Trinity Society,
which takes place in January, and
several committees were appointed by
the presiding officer. John Hadley
Doyle of the Holy Name Union, to
Immediately start the affair, and to
induce every Catholic man to affiliate
with the society.
Rev. Edward Magrath, S. J., spiritual
director of the society, made the prin
cipal talk of the evening, dwelling
principally on the good work accom
plished by Hoiy Name men in all com
munities, by living the Christ life as
an example for their neighbors'to fol
low. He urged all to be consistent to
the idea of the society, and to rally
to the standard of the order with the
same patriotism that governed their
zeal as Americans for the flag. He
spoke of the good work being accom
plished by Rev. Ignacius Smith as the
head of the Dominican headquarters
of the Holy Name Society in New
York.
The annual vesper service ;of the
Washington branch of the Holy Name
Society will take place on Holy Name
Sunday, January 4, In St. Paul's
Church at 7:30 o'clock'.
A two-week mission held by the
Jesuit fathers will start on the 4th of
January in Holy Trinity Church.
