JEWS OBSERVE FESTIVAL.
Celebration by Washington Jews of
"channukah," or the "festival of ded-
ication," commemorating one of the i
greatest victories of the ancient He-
brews, began at sunset last night
and will continue for eight days in I
Jewish homes and in the synagoguos. I
The occasion marks the day in 16?>
B.C.. when Greek resistance against
retention by the Hebrews of the
ancient temple at Jerusalem was
broken under the Jewish leadership
of Judah. the Maccabee. Kntering the
city of Jerusalem, the Jews entered
the Holy City, overturned the heathen
altars, "destroyed the idols, purified
the temple and rededicated it to the
worship of the one God. From this
the celebration is called the feast of
dedication, lasting for eight days.
The. principal ceremonial is the
kindling of lights in the homes, one
for the first night, two' the second
night and so on until the eight lights
are kindled on the eighth night.
