TAKOMA PARK WANTS
COMMUNITY BUILDING!
Speakers From Maryland Organi
zation Show Need of General
Meeting Place.
A town hall and community center
building is being demanded by the
citizens of Takoma Park. Md. At the
second of a series of town meetings,
held under the auspices of the coun
cil's committee on civic betterment,
speakers from nearly all of the four
teen organizations in the community
pointed out the long-felt need of a
suitable meeting place, not only for
the town council, but for the public
as a whole.
While the Takoma Park branch
library offers the use of its rooms
for public meetings, it is found un
suitable for many of the activities of
?he various organizations.
Another town meeting will be held
January 5. in the basement of the
Presbyterian Church.
The mayor and council at the reg
ular meeting Monday night last voted
to install a number of electric street
lights in the l'inecrest subdivision and
to purchase two steel plows for clean
ing the streets and sidewalks of snow
should any fall this winter.
The Takoma Hark Co-operative So
ciety, at a meeting held in the library
Wednesday night, voted to purchase
a lot on the corner of Carroll and Elm
avenues for the purpose of erecting
a store building. The Buying Club
announces the purchase of a carload
of Maine apples for distribution
among its members.
