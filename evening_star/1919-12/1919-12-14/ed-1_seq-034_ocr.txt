sent his case to the conference of
American Federation of Labor lead

a bill was introduced by Representa- J

to stabilize the purohasing power of
the American dollar. After listening

upon Congress the advisability of I
such an investigation, and the pur
pose of the meeting next Friday night
is for the representatives of the 30,000
members of the local union to fa

before voting on similar resolutions.
