Death of Charles H. Sauer.
Word has been received here from
Baltimore of the death of Charles H.
Sauer Monday at the home of his
daughter, 17 South Ellwood avenue.
He was ninety-one years old, and his
death was caused by the infirmities
of his advanced age.
Mr. Sauer was a former hotel owner
in this city, where he lived for more
than fifty years. His hotel was known
as the Sauer Hotel, and was located at
Pennsylvania avenue not far from the
Capitol. He retired about twelve years
ago and moved to Baltimore, where
he resided up to the time of his death.
He was a native of Denmark. Surviv
ing him are two daughters.
