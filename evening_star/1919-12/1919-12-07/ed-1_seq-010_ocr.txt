CHARLES N. THOMPSON DEAD.
Washington Educator Is Buried at
Hamilton, Va.
Charles Newton Thompson, a na
tive of Loundoun county, Va., but
for the past twenty-five years a resi
dent of the District, died at the Wash
ington Sanitarium Hospital December
2. He had a wide circle of friends,
particularly in the southwest section
of the city, where he held the position
of principal of the Jefferson School
for eighteen years.
He was widely known in education
al circles, being an alumnus of
George Washington Cniversity and a
member of the Federal Schoolmen's
Club.
Services were held at Lee's cbapel
