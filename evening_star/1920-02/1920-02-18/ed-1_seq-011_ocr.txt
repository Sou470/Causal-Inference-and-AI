Mr. Fess Proposes That Supreme

President's Status.
representative Fess, Ohio, chair
man of the republican congressional
committor, today announced he would
introduce a resolution proposing: a
constitutional amendment to define
the disability of a President to ad
minister his office and to authorize
the Vice President to assume his
duties.
Declaring that the Constitution is
silent on a definition of disability, Mr.
Fess said his measure would propose
that disability would be determined
by the Supreme Court when author
ized by a concurrent resolution of
Congress. In event Congress was r.ot
in session when the President be
came disabled, the Fess measure
would authorize the Vice President
to call a special session.
Mr. Fess said that while the con
dition of President Wilson "gave rise"
to his proposal, it was not designed
to cause his temporary removal from
office, and pointed out that the amend
ment. if adopted, would not bo effec
tive for several months.
