Anticipating the Market
The purposes of the recent advance in interest
rates by the Federal Reserve Banks are:
(1) To curb further speculation in com
modities as well as securities:
(2) To release funds, employed in unpro
ductive ventures, for the legitimate re
quirements of necessary business:
(3) To control the course of prices and re
duce their level to a more normal basis.
The steady advance in commodity prices in recent
years and the reduced purchasing power of the dollar,
have caused a decline in the values of fixed interest
bearing investments.
Conversely, with the progress of deflation in
the general situation, and the consequent
tendency toward declining pricee, the value
of the dollar and of HIGH-GRADE SECURI
TIES will advance.
We recommend to individuals. Trust Estates and
Savings Banks the purchase of such investments in
order to secure the benefit of the prevailing high
rates of interest over a long period of years and of the
natural increase in the value of principal and income
as normal prices ase restored.
We shall be glad to send our current list of care
fully selected securities which we recommend
for investment.
