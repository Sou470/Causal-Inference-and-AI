Salary schedules for the 107.000 federal

This was definitely stated at the head

Individual Rex alt a.
Owing to the fact that the great
principle of equalization of salaries will

greatly increased salaries, some but very
little increase, and some few may even I
get smaller salaries, it Is believed. The
nvynber of the last mentioned, however,
probably will be very small, if there are

It costs the government about $140,

reclassification commission, from sta
tistics in possession of the commis

Uncle Sam's salary budget, under

1140,000,000. it is definitely understood,

with any definite salary increase in

000.000. since this figure does not in
elude the bonus paid most employes

Feature of Wtrk.
The big point of the equalization
of salaries is to give all workers do
ing the same work the same salaries,
instead of allowing some workers on
a given line of endeavor to receive
as much as $1,000 more- than other
workers doing the same work.
