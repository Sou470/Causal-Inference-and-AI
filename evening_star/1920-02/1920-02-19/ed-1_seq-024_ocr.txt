More than a week has passed since Garry Herrmann relinquished i

Presidents Johnson and Heydler, who. under the terms of the

Harvey T. Woodruff, Chicago sporting writer, is regarded as hav-

Heydler may be unable to agree on any one of the official nominees,

One thing is assured, the man who is given the assignment will

commission undoubtedly are the questions of the length of term in

nates vary widely on these subjects. Many are adverse to granting a

selected might fail to give satisfaction as chairman. Proper remunera
tion also is a much-mooted topic, suggestions made by the club owners
ranging from $10,000 a year to three times that amount.
