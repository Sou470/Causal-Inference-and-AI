Child May Overlook One Item.
Perhaps one item that will be over

his essay is the fact that the man en
tering the Army is placed under the di
rection and supervision of an Army offi

secures from an enlistment in the
United States Army. The greater per
centage of Army officers are men in the
higher walk* of life, their education and
schooling of the best.
The enlisted man of the Army who de

carefully his company commander.
If ever>' soldier in the Army today
would make a sincere effort to pattern
after his company commander, there is
no doubt that he would secure one of
the greatest blessings that can come to
a man in his early period of manhood.
