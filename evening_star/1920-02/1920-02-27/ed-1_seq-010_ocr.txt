directing: Secretary George W. So Hers
to send a letter of condolence to the
parents of the late superintendent of
police.
W. J. Kerns, chairman of the com
mittee on ways and means, reported
he had been assured by members of
Congress that there will be no change
made in the bonus situation until
lifter the report of the reclassification
committee Is submitted.
Members of the association report
ed adversely on the proposed change

licemen. They favor the regular thirty
