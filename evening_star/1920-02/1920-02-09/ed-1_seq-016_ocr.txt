Now. for my personal return. The federal tax considers me the head of
tlie family, inasmuch as I make a home for my parents, both nearer eighty
than seventy. The only money besides my own drawing account mentioned
above coming into the house is my father's civil war pension, except occa
sionally a little extra earned from stocks. We live In New Jersey. How
should my return be filed?
55 A. I suggest that you set up a fiscal year basis, so that (Qnless your

federal report will be due from you until the end of your first fiscal year.
You will then he in a position to know whether or not, your drawings consist
of a withdrawal of earnings or whether the withdrawals were against bor
rowed capital.
