In her annual report Mrs. Blythe. the
treasurer, said the Woman's Evening
Clinic is self-supporting though an
nual dues and donations or members
and small fees paid by patients. Th?
804 members contribute about $2,400

tients last year amounted to $1,100.
The clinic has never had a deficit, and
now has about $4,000 in its treasury,

for enlargement of work. No appeal
has ever been made to the public for
funds, aside from an entertainment
given for the benefit of the fund for
enlargement of work, which netted
about $1,150.
