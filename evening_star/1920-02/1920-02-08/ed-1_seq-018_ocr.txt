U. S. MONEY IN MEXICO.
Circulation Exceeds That of Local
Government.
According to declarations made by
the Mexican minister of finance, re
ported today to the Department of
Commerce, the volume of American
money is greater than the amount of
Mexican money at present in circula
tion in Mexioo.
This is not only the case in silver
dollars, but also in fractional cur
rency, bills, gold and drafts. This is
the inevitable consequence of the lack
of Mexican money in circulation, a
dispatch states, and is due to the in
creasing transactions in various parts
of Mexico, necessitating the use of
American money to supply the de
flciency of the Mexican circulating
medium and to supplement it in pay
ments for exports from this counts
Another cause of the great use of
American money in Mexico is the in
numerable commercial trwMCtlonB
at frontier points and on the coasts,
where there are direct commercial re
lations with the United States, while
It is not denied that such a state of
affairs is hazardous, it is unavoidable
for the time being, in view of the fact
that if by any means American money
should disappear from circulation the
commerce and Industries of Mexico
would be paralyzed and transactions
and operations of all kinds would be
lacking. Therefore, there is no rem
edy but to continue as at present, re
serving until an opportune time the
necessary means to prevent falling
into a chaotic situation.
