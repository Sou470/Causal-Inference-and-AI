SEEKS TRADE SUBMITTAL
ON REBUILT TYPEWRITERS
About 200 manufacture? and deal
ers in second-hand, repaired and shop
and factory rebuilt typewriters from
all parts of the country have been in
vited to participate in a trade prac
tice submittal to be held at the Fed
eral Trade Commission next Friday at
10 a.m., the commission announced to
day.
The trade practice submittal an
nounced is a result of numerous ap
plications by members of the industry
to the commission for the issuance of
complaints against other members of
the same Industry. Various alleged
unfair practices in the rebuilt type
writer industry have been noted in
these applications. A brief summary
of the frequently occurring charges
Is that factory rebuilt machines are
sometimes sold as new machines; that
machines rebuilt In independent shops
are sold as having been factory re
built. and that machines which have
been merely overhauled or superficial
ly repaired have been sold as totally
rebuilt machines.
The commission hopes to be able at
the conference to eliminate harmful
practices which may hare developed.
Such a submittal will not mean the
quashing of the separate proceeding*
that are now on its docket.
