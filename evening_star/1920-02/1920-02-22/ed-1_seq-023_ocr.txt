ALEXANDRIA IS PLANNING
NEW BASE BALL LEAGUE
ALEXANDRIA. Va., February 21.?
Alexandria will have a live base ball
league this summer if the present
plans of several local lovers of the
national pastime materialize. Teams
are expected to he entered by the
Alexandria Community Service, the
Dreadnaught Athletic Club, the Vir
ginia Athletic Club, the Virginia
Shipbuilding Corporation and the
torpedo plant. This will put six
strong teams in the race for top hon
ors.
The athletic Held of the Virginia
Shipbuilding Corporation will be put
in condition and league games will
be played on this Held, as well as
on another local diamond. Present
plans call for the staging of double
headers Saturdays and Sundays, with
possibly games on Wednesdays.
A meeting to organize the league
will be held Friday night at the Com
munity Center, Prince and Royal
streets, and all interested are Invited
to attend.
