Shareholders of Grand Trunk
System Agree to Plan.
LONDON, February 21.?By the Asso
ciated Press.?The shareholders of the
Grand Trunk railway system, after
much discussion today, agreed to the
proposal to turn over the system, both
in Canada and the United States, to
the Canadian government.
A dozen dissenters held a meeting
later and adopted a resolution pro
testing against the terms accepted.
