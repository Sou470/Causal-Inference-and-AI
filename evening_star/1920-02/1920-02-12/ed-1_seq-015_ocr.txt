ANNIVERSARY IN 1922

Jubilee.
Brazil is planning to celebrate elab
orately the hundredth anniversary of
its .independence in April, 1922, and
El*?/. ih.? nat'ons of the world will
Partlc'Pate, according to
Franklin Adams, counselor of the
n Union. ^ho returned

Committees are working out fea

velopment of the country, while

de Janeiro, there will be local cele

of the moat interesting of which will

gal.
