WAS HIGHEST ON RECORD

sued today by the census bureau show
the death rate in the United States
in 1918 was the highest on record.
There were 1.471,367 deaths for the I
year, representing a rate of eighteen
per 1,000 population in the death regis
tration area of thirty states and twenty
seven cities, with a total estimated j
population of 81.s68.104.
More than 32 per cent, or 477,467,

monia. 380,996 having occurred in the I
last four months of the year, when
an epidemic of these diseases pre
vailed. The rate for influenza and

rates of 289.9 and 284.3 per 100.000.
