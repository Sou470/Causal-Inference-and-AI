was brought here for trial.

two men in Iowa, framing an alibi.

became apparent when the letter was
exposed to heat, was discoverd by
Jamas B. Green, a local handwriting
expert, who testified for the govern
ment.

sent were summoned here at the ex
pense of the government, and on their
arrival declined to aid Read. They
went on the witness stand and told
of the attempted "frame-up."'
